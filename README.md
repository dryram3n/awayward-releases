Windows, no installer, no account required.

1. **Download** the latest build with the button above, or pick a version from the [Releases page](https://github.com/dryram3n/awayward-releases/releases/latest).
2. **Unzip every file into one folder** and keep them together (the game ships alongside a few required DLLs, so do not move the .exe out on its own).
3. **Run `AwayWard.exe`** and start your adventure.

> **SmartScreen warning?** Windows may say the app is unrecognized (it is unsigned, not malware). Click **More info**, then **Run anyway**.
>
> **Black or empty window?** Update your graphics drivers first. If that does not help, double-click **"Run in Compatibility (ANGLE) mode.bat"** in the same folder to force a Direct3D 11 backend that runs on almost any GPU.

AwayWard tells you in-game when a new version is out, and your saves live in `%APPDATA%\Godot\app_userdata\AwayWard\saves`.

## What is AwayWard?

AwayWard is a genuine idle MMORPG that also plays fully offline. Your hero fights monsters automatically (even while the game is closed), earning gold, XP, gear and pets. Gear up, climb a seven-zone world map, summon companions, socket runewords, raise factions, and **rebirth** for permanent power.

A live backend powers real player-to-player systems: a cross-player market, co-op parties, shared factions, a server-wide world raid, a weekly community season, a house-showcase gallery, and a real leaderboard. Lose your connection (or flip on Offline Mode) and a persistent cast of simulated rivals keeps every screen playable. The online layer is co-op and trust-based, so the draw is the shared economy and community, not competitive ladders.

It began life as a Discord bot and grew into a full 2D game built with Godot 4.6.

## Highlights

- **Idle auto-combat** built on the original game's exact formulas: 96 enemies, elite and rare spawn variants, enemy packs, and offline progress that banks while you are away.
- **Deep loot and builds:** 6 rarities across 8 gear slots, procedural affixes, themed boss sets, and a Runeforge with 7 runewords, sockets, tempering and transmog.

## The online realm

Every online screen has a Realm/Online toggle and falls back to a local simulation when you are offline:

- **Leaderboard** ranked by power, level and rebirth
- **Player market** with cross-player consignment listings and atomic sales
- **Co-op parties** you join by code, shared right on the Hunt screen
- **Factions and clans** with pooled points, upgrades and research
- **The Convergence**, a server-wide co-op world raid with a shared HP pool and tiered spoils
- **House gallery** to publish your home and browse and like others
- A weekly **community season** that every player contributes toward

## Hands-on combat, when you want it

AwayWard is idle first, but two optional modes let you take the wheel:

- **Boss Duels:** when a zone boss appears, fight it by hand. Tap abilities on cue and dodge telegraphed windups for bonus loot. Lose and you simply keep your normal idle reward, so a duel is pure upside.
- **Trial Rifts:** an endless gauntlet of scaling waves with a weekly affix twist, plus a season depth leaderboard to climb.

Neither sells power. It is all skill and build flex.
